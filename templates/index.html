Overview

Startup Odisha, Government of Odisha brings an exclusive Investor Connect Series for the startups who are looking to raise funds for growing and scaling their business. The startups will be provided best-in-class mentoring from renowned investment leads, experts, angel investors and VCs. These sessions will cover a wide range of relevant topics for the startups to get acquaint with the funding ecosystem and get an outlook of the steps that they should follow before seeking funds. The participating startups will receive curated guidance on pitch perfection, investor readiness, legal compliance documentation and other necessary due diligence.

Programme Details

The Series consists of 20 Sessions that will start from 7th, June 2021. The schedule of dates, topics and speakers is notified in the program tab above.

The Participants must attend all the sessions to learn the art of pitching and get prepared for their first Master Pitch before the panel of investors.

Selected startups will get an opportunity to pitch during Mock Pitch Sessions, Demo Days and Investor Conclave in subsequent months.
